Anugunj Naman

I am an associate software engineer at Carelon, Hyderabad, India. I'm working with the Responsible AI Team on fairness, explainability and data drift in machine learning.

I graduated with a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Indian Institute of Information Technology, Guwahati in 2022.

Before this, I was a Research Intern at NVIDIA, where I worked on speech recognition, meta-learning, and few-shot learning. I was also a Research Intern at AI-ML-NLP Lab at IIT Patna, where I worked on multimodal learning.
