Anugunj Naman

I am a associate software engineer at Legato Health Technologies, Hyderabad, India. I'm working with Responsible AI Team on fairness, explainability and data drift for classification and regression models for tabular and text data.

My interest of research areas are in multimodal learning, few-shot learning, federated learning and fairness of machine learning models.

I graduated in Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Indian Institute of Information Technology, Guwahati in 2022.

Prior to this, I was a Research Intern at NVIDIA, where I worked on speech recognition, meta-learning and few-shot learning. I was also a Research Intern at AI-ML-NLP Lab at IIT Patna, where I worked on multimodal learning.
